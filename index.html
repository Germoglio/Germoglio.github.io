Intro

Hey, good to have you here! My name is Ian Germoglio, I am a 26 years old Brazilian Civil Engineer who has been traveling the world for work and education. By the way, check out my awesome work.

I have a very easy going personality, love sports, and recently decided to focus on learning how to code for different purposes. I've being coding for solving my engineering problems for at least 4 years. Mainly using MatLab and Python for data manipulation and analysis, but after graduating with a master's degree from the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign, I decided to dive deep into coding and devote most of my free time to learn back-end and front-end development
